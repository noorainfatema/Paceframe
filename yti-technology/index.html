Youth in Technology Incubator— Technology

An animated short used as an advertisement for Asian Americans for Community Involvement (AACI)’s Youth in Technology Incubator program. YTI hosts a variety of workshops and programs to teach youth about technology-related skills like building a personal website, editing multimedia, and more. For this animation, we wanted to lean into the website-building aspect specifically, incorporating elements of what a student might include in their own personal portfolio.

3d Design and Modeling: Nicole Garcia

3d Animation: Eric Ruperto

Music: Hans Galvez (HXNS)
